CULTURE

The culture of India is the way of living of the people of India's. India's languages, Religion, Dance, Music, Architecture, Food and Customs offer from place to place within the country. The Indian Culture, often labelled as an amalgamation of several cultures, spans across the Indian Subcontnent and has been influenced by a history that is several millennia old.
